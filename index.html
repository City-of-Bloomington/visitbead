Bloomington Entertainment & Arts District

An Economic Development Project of the City of Bloomington and a state designated Indiana Cultural District.

Mission

We seek to bring the business and creative sectors together to advance commerce and culture, build community, and spur economic development.

Our lively performance, gallery, and multi-use spaces are bustling year-round with exhibits, music, theater, comedy, dance, and festivals of all types.
